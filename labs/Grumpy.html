Grumpy Cat

Grumpy Cat(born April 4, 2012), Real Name Tarder Sauce,is a female cat and internet celebrity known for her grumpy facial expression. Her owner Tabatha Bundesen says that her pemanently grumpy-looking face is due to feline dwarfism. Grumpy cat's popularity originrated from a picture posted to the social news website reddit by Tabatha's brother Bryan on Septhember 22,2012. It was made in toan image macro with grumpy captions. "The offical Grumpy cat" on facebook has over 1.1 million likes. Grumpy cat was featured on the front page of The Wall street journal on May30,2013.
